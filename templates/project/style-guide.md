## Anti-AI Rules

- Avoid: generic connectors ("与此同时", "不禁", "显然" in bulk)
- Avoid: three-paragraph moralizing
- Prefer: concrete actions, concrete sensory anchors

## Wateriness Rules

- Every scene must change at least one of: goal, relationship, information, power.

## Forbidden List

- Do not reuse distinctive phrases from reference works.
- Do not reuse unique named entities.
